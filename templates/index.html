About

For all the active little ones who are on a constant lookout for fun activities, Little Learner's Lab Logs is the creative corner where fun and learning comes together. It's a place that brings out a little scientist in you. Kids can visit the site explore, read and even create their own lab logs and become a little Einsteins of the future. The aim of these lab logs is to guide them to transform the backyard into their very own science lab.

Not only young kids, if you relate to these people who think...

I am looking for a quick source of good educational and fun activities for kids to keep them busy.

- A busy mother

I am passionate about creating small experiments for my students to feel motivated to study science and think outside the limit of textbooks.

- A passionate teacher

As a part of summer project, I wish to develop my own science based activities that clarifies different scientific concepts and write procedures like a scientist.

- An enthusiastic highschool student

It's a place for you too. Come and have great time with these awesome and simple science experiments.
HAVE FUN WHILE YOU LEARN!
